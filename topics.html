Memory & Nostalgia

Warmth, loss, reflection, comfort, regrets

  • What’s a memory you wish you could relive? Why?
  • Do you think memories change over time, or do we change around them?
  • What sound, smell, or image brings you back to a different version of yourself?
  • If you could forget one memory, would you?
  • How does nostalgia feel in your body?
