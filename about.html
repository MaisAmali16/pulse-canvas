About Pulse Canvas

Pulse Canvas explores how conversations change when we can sense another person’s internal state. Two participants speak through an emotionally focused topic while biometric signals support reflection. The goal is not to “diagnose” emotion, but to create a gentler space for listening, empathy, and awareness.

What Makes It Unique

Instead of showing you your own data, Pulse Canvas emphasizes relational experience: you encounter the other person through their sound, rhythm, and abstract visual output.
