THE VISION

In a world of superficial digital interactions, Pulse Canvas offers a return to profound human connection. It is a synchronized biometric art experience that bridges the gap between what we say and what we truly feel.

1. The Biometric Mirror

Using cutting-edge browser technology, the canvas reads three vital inputs in real-time: your heart rate, your facial expressions, and the intensity of your voice. These raw human signals are instantly translated into a living, breathing digital painting.

2. Curated Vulnerability

To guide the experience, our system generates a unique, emotionally focused conversation topic for you and your partner. As you navigate the prompt, the artwork dynamically shifts, reacting to the ebb and flow of your shared vulnerability.

3. Empathy Through Art

The goal is not to "diagnose" emotion, but to create a gentler space for active listening. By removing clinical data and replacing it with abstract sound and rhythm, we allow participants to actually see and hear how their words affect another person.
