KEEP IT GOING

Use these optional “conversation boosters” if the chat feels awkward or stops too early.
They’re just here if you want help keeping the flow.

1. Pace & Flow

Take turns: Let one person go first, then switch.

Bridge thoughts: "That reminds me of..." or "A related thing is..."

2. Dig Deeper

Ask follow-ups: "What led to that?" or "What happened next?"

Get specific: Add details to paint a clearer picture of your experience.

3. Find Connection

Clarify meaning: "When you say ___, what do you mean?"

Compare: "Is yours similar or different?" or "What part do you relate to?"
