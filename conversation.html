Keep It Going

Use these optional “conversation boosters” if the chat feels awkward or stops too early. You don’t have to use them. They’re just here if you want help keeping the flow.

  1. Take turns. Let one person go first, then switch.
  2. Ask a follow-up. “What led to that?” “What happened next?” “How did that change things?”
  3. Get specific. Add detail to make it clearer.
  4. Clarify meaning. “When you say ___, what do you mean?” “What does that look like for you?”
  5. Compare & connect. “Is yours similar or different?” “What part do you relate to?”
  6. Bridge to the next thought. “That reminds me of…” “A related thing is…”
