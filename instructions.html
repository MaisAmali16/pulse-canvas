PREPARATION

Pulse Canvas reads your heartbeat, voice, and facial expressions. Let’s make sure your environment is ready.

SYSTEM CHECKLIST

  • 1. Partner Up: Choose someone to experience this with.
  • 2. Tech Check: Use a laptop or desktop computer with a working camera and microphone.
  • 3. Audio Quality: Please wear headphones. This prevents your microphone from echoing the other person's voice.
  • 4. The Sensor: When prompted on the final screen, place your finger securely on the pulse sensor.
