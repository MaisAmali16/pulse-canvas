Welcome to Pulse Canvas

This experience guides two participants through an emotionally focused conversation supported by heartbeat-driven art and sound.

Before You Start

  • Choose a partner (someone you know or do not know).
  • Enter the booth.
  • Put on the headphones and speak into the microphone.
  • There is a pulse sensor placed in front of you.
  • When prompted, place your finger on the sensor to begin the reading.
