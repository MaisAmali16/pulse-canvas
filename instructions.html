Welcome to Pulse Canvas

Pulse Canvas is a two-person conversation experience supported by real-time visuals. The experience responds to heartbeat input, voice audio, and camera-based facial tracking.

Before You Start

  • Choose a partner (someone you know or don’t know).
  • Use a laptop/desktop if possible (recommended for a smoother experience).
  • Make sure your device has a working microphone and camera.
  • Have headphones available so you can hear each other clearly.
  • When prompted, place your finger on the pulse sensor to begin the reading.
